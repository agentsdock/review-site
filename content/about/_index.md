## Our Mission

At TestedHarbor, our mission is simple: help you find the best products without the guesswork. We believe that honest, thorough product testing should be available to everyone.

## How We Work

Every product we review goes through a rigorous evaluation process. We buy every product ourselves, test them in real-world conditions, and publish our findings without any influence from manufacturers.

### Our Values

**Transparency**: We disclose our testing methods and are upfront about how we earn money through affiliate links. Our reviews are never influenced by sponsorships or free products.

**Thoroughness**: We spend weeks testing each product, evaluating performance, durability, ease of use, and value for money. No detail is overlooked.

**Independence**: We buy every product we test. We accept no free products from manufacturers and never allow sponsors to influence our ratings or recommendations.

**Customer Focus**: Our reviews are written for you, the buyer. We focus on the features and performance aspects that actually matter in daily use.

## Our Team

TestedHarbor is run by a team of product enthusiasts, engineers, and writers who are passionate about helping people make informed purchasing decisions.

## How We Make Money

We participate in affiliate programs including Amazon Associates. When you click on a product link on our site and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. This is how we fund our testing and keep our content free.

We never accept payment for positive reviews, and our affiliate relationships do not influence our product ratings or recommendations.
