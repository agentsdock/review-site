## Our Testing Philosophy

At TestedHarbor, we believe that great product recommendations come from rigorous, unbiased testing. Here is exactly how we evaluate every product.

## Step 1: Research and Selection

Before we buy any product, we conduct extensive market research:

- Analyze customer reviews across multiple platforms
- Study expert opinions and industry reports
- Identify the most popular and highly-rated products in each category
- Ensure we cover a range of price points

## Step 2: Purchase

We buy every product we test using our own money. We do not accept free products from manufacturers. This ensures complete independence in our evaluations.

## Step 3: Hands-On Testing

Each product is tested in real-world conditions for a minimum of two weeks. Our testing covers:

- **Performance**: Does the product do what it claims?
- **Durability**: How well does it hold up over time?
- **Ease of Use**: Is it intuitive and convenient?
- **Value**: Is it worth the price?

## Step 4: Comparison

We compare all tested products side by side using standardized scoring criteria. This allows us to identify the best products in each category and price range.

## Step 5: Writing

Our reviews are written to give you all the information you need to make a confident decision. We highlight both strengths and weaknesses of every product.

## Our Commitment

- We never accept payment for positive reviews
- We buy every product we test
- We update our reviews regularly as new products are released
- We disclose our affiliate relationships transparently
